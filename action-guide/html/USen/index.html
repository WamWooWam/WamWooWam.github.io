View map
Press to open the map and check your current location or destination. If while viewing the map you tilt and select a Checkpoint Flag, you can move right to it. You can also check various lists from here, including the Power Moon List and Capture List, by pressing .
Assist Mode
Press to open the pause menu and select Options and then Choose Mode to activate Assist Mode.
In this mode you'll have extra hearts, which can be pretty handy if you need help. Other features include one that will allow you to quickly resume play if you fall somewhere dangerous, one that restores your life if you stand still, and an arrow that will show you which way to go.

amiibo 1
Any amiibo will get you a hint about Moon locations from Uncle amiibo, but certain amiibo will get you special costumes. So be sure to pay him a visit!

Uncle amiibo

amiibo 2
Joy-Con (R) Nintendo Switch Pro Controller
If you have the Mario, Peach, or Bowser amiibo, hold and touch the amiibo to the NFC area of the controller. You can try other amiibo to see what you get, too!
Mario amiibo: Become invincible for a certain amount of time.
Peach amiibo: Get a Life-Up Heart.
Bowser amiibo: Shows the locations of regional coins. Rotate the camera to look for them.
Ranking
You can check out the Ranking Board if you're connected to the Internet. You can check rankings for all sorts of different scores and times, and even compete among your friends or the whole world!
Snapshot
Press to ready a snapshot. You can position the camera, add a filter, and snap a photo to show off your style! When you're ready, press to take the shot, and it will be saved in your system's album .
